Banana Boat

A Banana Boat is an inflatable watercraft, often yellow and shaped like its namesake fruit. Designed for fun, it skims across water as passengers hold on, creating laughter-filled memories. Perfect for group activities, it's a thrilling blend of excitement and challenge.
