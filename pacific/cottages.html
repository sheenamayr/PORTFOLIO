Cottages at Pacific Trails

What is a cottage?

A cottage is a small cosy residential dwelling usually found in, but not limited to, the countryside. A cottage is a small house, particularly a traditional or old-fashioned house, or one that is used seasonally. Your family might rent a cottage near the beach every summer.
